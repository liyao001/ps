About me

As a computational biologist, I am interested in tackling complex biological questions by designing and implementing cutting-edge computational solutions. My work often involves analyzing multi-omics data to uncover the hidden regulatory landscape of the genome, locating and characterizing elements like enhancers that are vital for gene control and were previously unknown. My expertise spans statistical modeling, machine learning, deep learning, and full-stack development, enabling me to create robust bioinformatics tools from concept to deployment to achieve these discoveries.
