DEconvolution of Tissue profiles with Accurate Interpretation of Locus-specific Signals

<p>DeepDETAILS is a novel deep-learning framework that reconstructs cell-type-specific genomic signals from bulk tissue data with base-pair precision. Its core innovation is the use of readily available single-cell ATAC-seq data as a reference to deconvolve various other genomic datasets, such as those from nascent transcript sequencing or ChIP-seq.</p>
